Under this scheme, two children of registered workers between the ages of 6 and 16 receive free residential education in dedicated schools. The UP Atal Awasiya Vidyalaya Yojana is a scheme that provides free education to poor children of construction workers in Uttar Pradesh. The scheme is currently being implemented in 18 districts of the state.
