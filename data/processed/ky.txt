In this scheme, if the farmer purchases farming equipment from an authorized dealer, then the state government gives a subsidy to the farmers which is 40% of the total price. Up to 50% subsidy is given to government farmers. Payment of grantPhysical verification of the instrument will be done by the Agriculture Supervisor/Assistant Agriculture Officer.Purchase bill will have to be presented at the time of verification.The grant will be paid online into the farmer's bank account.BenefitsGrant Details : Details of grant payable scheme-wise and category-wise of instruments S.
