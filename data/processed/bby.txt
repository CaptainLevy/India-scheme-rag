This scheme helps those who are educated and talented but cannot generate enough income to apply for government jobs or other jobs due to the financial crisis. BenefitsBenefitsThe government will provide financial assistance of Rs. 1,000 to Rs. 1,500 till the time youths get desired job.Private and government jobs are available on one portal.Online application facility.Facility to search jobs by category, location, department, and salary.EligibilityEligibilityThe candidate must be a permanent resident of Uttar Pradesh.Candidates must have passed at-least 10th standard (High School) examinations.The candidate must be currently unemployed i.e he must not be not working in any private or government job.Moreover, all the applicants must lie in the age group between 21-35 years.The total family income of the candidates from all sources must not exceed Rs. 3 lahks per annum.Application ProcessOnlineApplication ProcessFirstly visit the official website. On the homepage, click the New Account link present in the main menu or directly click here Now fill out the registration form with the required details and submit.Now log in to fill out the UP Berojgari Bhatta Yojana Online Application Form and complete their profile. After submission of the application form and profile completion, click at the Submit button to forward it to the concerned authorities for approval.Documents RequiredList of the required documents Residential proof.Aadhar card.Income certificate.Birth certificate.Caste certificate (if eligible).Notary-certified Affidavit.Registration certificate of employment exchange.Proof of educational qualification (mark sheets, certificates, etc.).Frequently Asked QuestionsWhat is UP Berojgari Bhatta Yojna ?This scheme helps those who are educated and talented but cannot generate enough income to apply for government jobs or other jobs due to the financial crisis. What are the benefits ?1. The government will provide financial assistance of Rs. 1,000 to Rs. 1,500 till the time youths get their desired jobs. 2. Private and government jobs are available on one portal. 3. Online application facility. 4 .Facility to search jobs by category, location, department, and salary.What is the Eligibility ?1. The candidate must be a permanent resident of Uttar Pradesh. 2. Candidates must have passed at-least 10th standard (High School) examinations. 3. The candidate must be currently unemployed i.e he must not be not working in any private or government job. 4. Moreover, all the applicants must lie in the age group between 21-35 years. 5. The total family income of the candidates from all sources must not exceed Rs. 3 lahks per annum.How to apply online ?1. Firstly visit the official website https://sewayojan.up.nic.in/ 2. On the homepage, click the New Account link present in the main menu. 3. Now fill out the registration form with the required details and submit. 4. Now log in to fill out the UP Berojgari Bhatta Yojana Online Application Form and complete their profile. 5. After submission of the application form and profile completion, click at the Submit button to forward it to the concerned authorities for approval.List of the required documents 1. Residential proof. 2. Aadhar card. 3. Income certificate. 4. Birth certificate. 5. Caste certificate (if eligible). 6. Notary-certified Affidavit. 7. Registration certificate of employment exchange. 8.
