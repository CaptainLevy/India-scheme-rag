Students enrolled in Colleges/ Universities/Academic Institutions or those who passed out recently can apply. The duration of the internship can be 1-month or 2-months. The selection of the Interns would be made on the merit of academic performance and/or personal interview. Selected Interns would be paid an honorarium of 5000/- per month. MWCD would award an Internship Certificate to the students on successful completion of the Internship for the said period. The Activities for Interns during this period will be as follows:1. Participation in planning and research-related activities. Students are expected to undertake short-duration pilot studies/surveys besides undertaking an evaluation of MWCD schemes on the issues identified and justified by them or issues listed by the Ministry from its areas of interest/work.2. To provide inputs for policy formulation/scheme implementation through empirical research work with the Ministry to supplement the process of policy formulation, implementation of schemes, and other initiatives related to women and children.3. Participation in-country Seminars/Workshops or interaction at any such platforms that MWCD may find relevant for young students/scholars for exposure in the field of gender issues and children. BenefitsHonorarium:Selected Interns would be paid an honorarium of 5000/- per month. Certificate:MWCD would award an Internship Certificate to the students on successful completion of the Internship for the said period.EligibilityStudents enrolled in Colleges/ Universities/Academic Institutions or those who passed out recently can apply. For short-term (one month) Internship, students who are doing their undergraduate courses in any subject.The Internship of 2-months is open for those candidates who are holding 2nd class graduation Degree and are enrolled further for Post Graduation Courses in the field of Social Sciences/Social work, Nutrition Child and Gender Studies, Rural Development, Home Science, Economics, Psychology, Sociology, Anthropology, and Political Science are eligible to apply. Students from Management/IITs/IT sectors would also be eligible to apply.ExclusionsNAApplication ProcessOnlineInterested students/scholars may apply for an internship in the Ministry by downloading the Form given in Annexures I & 2, and forwarding the filled-up form to: intem.wcd@nic.in Applications received after the last date will not be considered. For any further details/queries, the following officer may be contacted:Mr. D.K.Tiwary, Coordinator (Internship Scheme)Ministry of Women and Child DevelopmentGovernment of IndiaStatistics Bureau, 3rd Floor, Jeevan Vihar Building Parliament Street, New Delhi-110001Email: intern.wcd@nic.in Documents RequiredPassport Sized PhotographAadhaar CardProof of IdentityProof of Age / DOBProof of AddressProof reg. the Educational QualificationsRecommendations of the Institutions where the student is enrolled for academic pursuitDeclaration of secrecy before reporting for the internshipUndertaking giving the right to the Ministry to cancel the Internship for non performance during the Internship period as may be deemed/observed by the Ministry. NOTE: Selected interns would declare that he/ she understands that the offer of the internship by the Ministry of WCD is not an offer of any job or any commitment of any future job, hence the offer of internship would not be used as a claim for that matter. Frequently Asked QuestionsWhat Is The Amount Of Honorarium That Will Be Paid To The Interns?Selected Interns would be paid an honorarium of 5000/- per month.Will An Internship Certificate Also Be Provided To The Interns?Yes, MWCD would award an Internship Certificate to the students on successful completion of the Internship for the said period.What Documents Will Be Required To Be Furnished In Order To Apply For This Internship?Passport Sized Photograph, Aadhaar Card, Proof of Identity, Proof of Age / DOB, Proof of Address, Proof reg. the Educational Qualifications, Recommendations of the Institutions where the student is enrolled for academic pursuit, Declaration of secrecy before reporting for the internship, Undertaking giving the right to the Ministry to cancel the Internship for non-performance during the Internship period as may be deemed/observed by the Ministry.Can The Offer Of The Internship By The Ministry Of WCD Be Considered As An Offer Of Any Job Or Any Commitment Of Any Future Job?No, the selected interns would declare that he/ she understands that the offer of the internship by the Ministry of WCD is not an offer of any job or any commitment of any future job, hence the offer of internship would not be used as a claim for that matter.
