Financial Assistance for Purchase of Stake Net and its Accessories OkYou need to before applying for schemesCancelSign In GoaFinancial Assistance for Purchase of Stake Net and its AccessoriesFinancial AssistanceFishFishermenSubsidyDetails Financial Assistance for Purchase of Stake Net and its Accessories is a scheme by the Department of Fisheries, Government of Goa. Stake net is a traditional method of catching fish practiced by the fishermen residing near the bank of the river. Under this scheme, financial assistance is provided in the form of a subsidy on the purchase of stake nets and accessories for the fishermen. The applications are accepted only in offline mode.BenefitsFor General CategorySubsidy of 50%, limited to 30,000 on a unit cost of 60,000 (excluding GST).For SC/ST/Woman CategorySubsidy of 60%, limited to 36,000 on a unit cost of 60,000 (excluding GST).NOTE: The beneficiary can avail of the scheme every 3 years.EligibilityThe applicant should be a traditional fisherman by birth or profession.The applicant should be a resident of Goa.The applicant should have purchased a Stake Net and its Accessories.Application ProcessOfflineStep 1: The interested applicant should request the prescribed format of the application form from the department by paying 50.Step 2: In the application form, fill in all the mandatory fields, paste the passport-sized photograph (signed across, if required), and attach copies of all the mandatory documents (self-attest, if required).Step 3: Submit the duly filled and signed application form along with the documents to the concerned authority at the Department of Fisheries, Dayanand Bandodkar Marg, Patto Colony, Panaji Goa - 403 521, or to the official posted at BDO Office/ Sub-Offices of the Department of Fisheries. Grievance RedressalAny dissatisfaction or complaint about any service or its standards can be registered with the Department's Grievance Redressal Officer. The grievance can also be lodged on the link dir-fish.goa@nic.in. All complaints will be acknowledged by us and final action taken will be communicated within 30 days.Documents Required1. Stake Allotment Permission issued by the Department of Fisheries.2. Stake Net Registration Certificate and License Certificate issued by the Department of Fisheries.3. Original Invoice from Authorized Dealers for the Purchase of Stake Net and its Accessories.4. Undertaking as per Proforma.5. Copy of Aadhaar Card.6. Mandate Form along with Bank Pass Book.Frequently Asked QuestionsWhere should the duly filled application form and documents be submitted?Submit the documents to the concerned authority at the Department of Fisheries, Dayanand Bandodkar Marg, Patto Colony, Panaji Goa - 403 521, or to the official at BDO Office/ Sub-Offices of the Department of Fisheries. How often can a beneficiary avail of the scheme, and what is the subsidy percentage for the SC/ST/Woman category?The scheme can be availed every 3 years, and the subsidy for SC/ST/Woman category is 60%, limited to 36,000. What is the unit cost of a Stake Net and its Accessories under the "Financial Assistance for Purchase of Stake Net and its Accessories" scheme?The unit cost of a Stake Net and its Accessories is 60,000 (excluding GST). What is the significance of the Stake Allotment Permission, and how can one obtain it?The Stake Allotment Permission is essential and can be acquired from the Department of Fisheries, Goa, prior to applying for the scheme. Can the subsidy amount be utilized for any other purpose apart from the purchase of Stake Net and its Accessories?The subsidy is specifically designated for the purchase of Stake Net and its Accessories and cannot be diverted for any other purpose. Is there any provision for extending the deadline for submitting applications, and are there age-related criteria for applicants?There is no deadline for applications, and there are no age-related criteria. Applications can be submitted without any time constraints. How is the subsidy amount disbursed to the beneficiaries, and what is the mode of payment?The approved subsidy amount is disbursed directly to the beneficiary's bank account through electronic transfer. In the event of a change in the unit cost after application submission, how is the subsidy recalculated?The subsidy is fixed based on the unit cost at the time of application submission, and any subsequent changes do not impact the approved subsidy amount. If a beneficiary has already availed of the scheme, can they reapply for another unit before the stipulated three-year gap?No, the scheme allows beneficiaries to apply every 3 years. Reapplication before this period is not permitted. Can the subsidy be availed for multiple units of Stake Nets in a single application, and what are the considerations for such cases?The subsidy is applicable per unit, and multiple units can be covered in a single application, subject to meeting eligibility and documentation requirements. What is the process for calculating the subsidy amount, considering the unit cost and applicable percentage?The subsidy is calculated as a percentage of the unit cost. For the General category, it's 50%, limited to 30,000, and for SC/ST/Woman category, it's 60%, limited to 36,000. In situations where the applicant's address changes after submission, how should they communicate this to the department?Applicants should promptly inform the department in writing about any changes in their address, providing updated documentation as necessary.
