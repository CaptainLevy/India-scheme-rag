This scheme is available for the senior citizens of the Uttarakhand state minimum age 60 years. Objective The main objective of this scheme is to help the old people financially, only those who are more than 60 years of age and destitute can take advantage of this scheme. Older people have to face many problems due to old age. They have to depend on others to live their life, so the government has given financial help to these people and to make them self-reliant. Old age pension scheme Uttarakhand Has been started so that more and more old people can be helped financially. BenefitsBenefitsOld people can improve their financial condition by taking advantage of this scheme.The BPL beneficiaries of the state who are destitute old people will be provided with the benefit of this scheme.Older people will not have to depend on others to live their life.Uttrakhand Vridhawstha Pension Yojana Under this, people who are 60 to 79 years old are given a pension of Rs 200 from the central government and Rs 1300 from the state government i.e., Rs 1500.Similarly, a total pension of Rs 1500 (Rs 500/- per month by the Central Government and Rs 1000/- per month by the State Government) is given to BPL beneficiaries above the age of 80 years. This scheme is like a support for the old people of the state. EligibilityEligibility The applicant should be a permanent resident of Uttarakhand.Only people who are 60 years or more of the state can apply for this scheme.The beneficiary should be from a BPL family or his monthly income should not exceed Rs.4000.The beneficiary whose son or son is more than 20 years of age but the candidate is living below the poverty line is eligible for this scheme.The beneficiary over 60 years of age but not belong to a BPL family will be given the entire pension amount by the state government.Application ProcessOnlineApplication ProcessFirst of all, go to the official website. Download the application form. Fill all the information sought in the form and after filling it will have to be signed by the Village Head and Village Panchayat Development Officer (VDO).Attach all the necessary documents and submit the form to the Social Welfare Department. Documents RequiredList of the required documentsAADHAR Card.BPL Ration Card.Income Certificate.Domicile Certificate.Birth Certificate.Address Proof.Frequently Asked QuestionsTo whom old age pension can be sanctioned?Any woman or man above 60 years of age who belongs to BPL family or whose annual income from all sources does not exceed Rs.12000/- and land in rural area does not exceed two and a half acres, old age pension can be approved.Who issues income certificate for old age pension approval?Income certificate is issued by Tehsildar of concerned Tehsil.Who has the right to accept old age pension?Right to accept old age pension In rural areas, the eligible person is selected in the open meeting of the Gram Panchayat and in urban areas, the Sub-Divisional Officer of the concerned Tehsil has the right to accept the pensionWhat is the period for payment of old age pension?The amount of old age pension is paid every 3 months.Can old age pension be given to both husband and wife?Only husband and wife of BPL family can be sanctioned old age pension. In case of acceptance of pension on the basis of income certificate, only one of the husband or wife can be sanctioned pension, on this also priority will be given to the female candidate.Can old age pension be given even if the person belonging to BPL family has a son/grandson who is above 20 years of age?Those whose sons or grandsons are 20 years of age or more, but they are living below the poverty line, can be given old age pension.When is the new pension sanctioned?Pension to new beneficiaries is approved only twice in a year, in January and July respectively, i.e., applications received from the month of January to June are accepted from the month of July and those received from July to December are accepted from the month of JanuaryIs income certificate necessary for BPL number holders?There is no need for income certificate for pension approval for BPL number holdersList of required documents1.
