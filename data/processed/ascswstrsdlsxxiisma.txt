Under the scheme, the Scheduled Castes Category students who have Secured Top Ranks at the State and District Levels in Gujarat in Standard X & XII are given cash prizes. Cash prizes are awarded to the first three students who secure the highest ranks in both Standard X and Standard XII across all streams.BenefitsAward/Cash Prizes Details at State Level:Sl. What are the award details for state-level rankers?For Standard X, the first three students receive 41,000/-, 21,000/-, and 11,000/-. For Standard XII, the prizes are 31,000/-, 21,000/-, and 11,000/- respectively.What are the award details for district- level rankers?For Standard X, the first three students receive 6,000/-, 5,000/-, and 4,000/-. For Standard XII, the prizes are 6,000/-, 5,000/-, and 4,000/- respectively.Who is eligible for these cash prizes?Students belonging to the Scheduled Castes Category of Gujarat who secure top ranks at both state and district levels in Standard X & XII exams are eligible.Are there any income limits for eligibility?No, there is no income limit for eligibility under this scheme.How can students avail the benefits of this scheme?The list of eligible students is obtained from the Gujarat Secondary and Higher Secondary Board, and cash awards are given to selected students accordingly.How are the winners selected?Winners are selected based on their rankings provided by the Gujarat Secondary and Higher Secondary Board.Sources And ReferencesOfficial WebsiteScheme Details (Page No. 85)NotificationOkWas this helpful?News and UpdatesNo new news and updates availableShare OkYou need to before applying for schemesCancelSign In GujaratAward to Scheduled Castes Students Who Secured Top Ranks at State and District Level in Standard X & XII (Sahuji Maharaj Assistance)AwardCash PrizeSCStudentTop RankDetailsBenefitsEligibilityApplication ProcessDocuments RequiredFrequently Asked QuestionsThe scheme Award to Scheduled Castes Students Who Secured Top Ranks at State and District Level in Standard X & XII is initiated by the Director Scheduled Caste Welfare, Department of Social Justice & Empowerment, Government of Gujarat.
