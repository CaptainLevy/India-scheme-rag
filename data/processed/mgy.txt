The scheme was launched in the year 1995. The scheme offers financial assistance to Scheduled Caste individuals who wish to establish cottage industries for self-employment without requiring bank loans. Additionally, employment/tool kits are provided to those who seek self-employment opportunities. In the limit of the list according to 28 trades, the necessary toolkits purchased and distributed by GRIMCO in the form of necessary resources/tools for business, this scheme is for the improvement of the economic condition of individual artisans, who are living below the poverty line and to increase the income of weaker section of society, small scale trade/business like vendors, vegetable seller,s and carpentry.BenefitsUnder the scheme, tool kits are provided for a total of 28 types of trades/businesses to individuals seeking self-employment opportunities. Step 07: After updating the profile successfully, select the scheme appearing on the home page; an application form will open.Step 08: Fill in all the mandatory details in the application form and upload all the relevant documents.Step 09: Now agree with the terms & conditions and click on Save Application .Step 10: Submit the application form and take out the print of the application for future reference. The applicant can note down the application number to track the application status by logging into the portal using their User ID and Password. However, there is no income limit for applicants belonging to the Most Backward Caste among the Scheduled Castes.Can previous beneficiaries or their family members reapply for the scheme?No, if the beneficiary or any family members have already availed benefits under the scheme, they are not eligible for reapplication.Where can eligible applicants apply for the scheme?Eligible applicants can apply through the e- Samaj Kalyan Portal: https://esamajkalyan.gujarat.gov.in/How many types of trades/businesses are covered under the scheme?A total of 28 types of trades/businesses are covered under the scheme, ranging from handicraft work to mobile repairing.Can applicants from outside Gujarat apply for the scheme?No, only native residents of Gujarat are eligible to apply for the "Manav Garima Yojana" scheme.What is the significance of providing employment/tool kits?Providing employment/tool kits ensures that individuals have the necessary resources to start their businesses, reducing dependency on external sources like bank loans.Sources And ReferencesOfficial WebsiteScheme Details (Page No. 97)Scheme DetailsE-Samaj Kalyan PortalApplication FormNotification-1Notification-2OkWas this helpful?News and UpdatesNo new news and updates availableShare OkYou need to before applying for schemesCancelSign In GujaratManav Garima YojanaSCSelf-employmentTool KitsTradeDetailsBenefitsEligibilityApplication ProcessDocuments RequiredFrequently Asked QuestionsThe scheme Manav Garima Yojana was launched by the Director Scheduled Caste Welfare, Department of Social Justice & Empowerment, Government of Gujarat.
