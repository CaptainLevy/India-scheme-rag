This scheme is for those widows who have decided to start a new life after the time spent with their former husbands. The government provides a certain amount to widows, which can be used in the marriage of widows. The money is used to help widows meet the comfortable needs of their new home.BenefitsFinancial Assistance/Gift amount 51,000/-EligibilityApplicant must be a widow women from Rajasthan State.Applicant must be resident of Rajasthan or living in Rajasthan for 3 years before re- marriage.Applicant should be entitled to pension as per widow pension rules.Applicant age should be between 18-50 years.If the applicant has remarried before this scheme then he/she will not be eligible.Application ProcessOfflineApplicant need to get the application form from :Officer of the District Social Justice and Empowerment Department.Download from the official document page no. 7.On plain paper. Submit to the Officer of the District Social Justice and Empowerment Department.Attach all the required documentsAge proof (school certificate, birth certificate)Husband's death certificateResidence proofIncome proof (all sources)Details of family membersWidow pension document (if applicable)Remarriage certificate (certified copy) Documents RequiredThe applicant must have the following documents to apply for Widow Marriage Gift Scheme :-Aadhar Card CopyAddress Proof CopyBank Passbook CopyBirth Certificate Copy : For age proofDeath Certificate Copy : of HusbandJan Aadhar/Bhamashah Card CopyPassport Size PhotoFrequently Asked QuestionsWhere to apply for Vidhwa Vivah Uphar Yojana Rajasthan?The Rajasthan government's Social Justice and Empowerment Department launched the "Vidhva Vivah Uphar Yojana" program, a social welfare initiative that aims to improve the lives of widow women. What benefits are provided under the scheme?Financial support is provided.May Other State persons also apply for this scheme?No, only for Rajasthan.What documents are required ?The applicant must have the following documents: 1. Aadhar Card Copy. 2. Address Proof Copy. 3. Bank Passbook Copy. 4. Birth Certificate Copy : For age proof. 5. Death Certificate Cop : of Husband. 6. Jan Aadhar/Bhamashah Card Copy. 7. Passport Size Photo.What is Bhamashah Card?The Bhamashah card provided by this yojana is linked to a bank account in the name of the housewife. The card also provides women with biometric identification and core banking functions, as well as a variety of cash benefits that are directly transferred to their bank account.Is this scheme only for Widows?YesWhat is DBT?DBT stands for Direct Benefit Transfer.
