Provided e, Identification of sporting talent among students all over the country in the age group of 8 12 years (for admission in Class IV to Class VI) who possess inborn qualities such as anthropometric, physical and physiological capabilities without any anatomical infirmities.In the second phase, Nurturing of the Sporting Potential talent identified in District level Sports schools / Central Sports Schools / Junior Sports Academies etc., to make them excel at the National and international sports competitions. ObjectiveIdentify budding athletes with exceptional potential. Provide them with quality training and development opportunities. Prepare them for future success in national and international competitions BenefitsThe program will help in finding athletes with natural abilities like anthropometric, physical, and physiological capacities without any anatomical defects among children for admission in Class IV to Class VI.The program will support the growth of Indian sports, especially rural sports.Sports schools at the district, regional, and national levels as well as national and international sports academies will foster the development of athletes sporting potential and talent. As a result of this, the number of athletes in the nation will increase. EligibilityThe applicant should be a citizen of India.The applicant's age should be between the ages of 8 and 12 years. The applicant is only available to participants who complete the physical efficiency and sports aptitude tests.The program allows players from any Indian state to register. As a result, candidates can obtainthe application should be known in both Hindi and English as well as other regional languages. After six months, students who are unable to register for the program or who are declared ineligible by the program might choose to reapply.Application ProcessOnlineStep-1: Go to the Official Website of the National Talent Search Portal Step-2: Now click New Registration and provide the below details - First Name. - Last Name. - Gender. - Date of Birth. - Email Address. - Mobile Number. - Aadhaar Number. - Click on register now.Step-3: After the registration, login again.Step-4: Create a profile and update it.Step-5: Under the profile, search the scheme name and apply. Documents RequiredAge Proof.Aadhaar Card.Medical certificate.Photograph.Frequently Asked QuestionsWhat is the National Sports Talent Search (NSTS)?The National Sports Talent Search is a government initiative in India aimed at identifying and nurturing young sporting talent across various disciplines.Who organizes the National Sports Talent Search?The initiative is usually organized by the Ministry of Youth Affairs and Sports, Government of India, in collaboration with sports authorities and federations.What is the primary objective of the National Sports Talent Search program?The primary goal is to identify and nurture young sporting talent at the grassroots level, providing them with the necessary support and opportunities to excel in their chosen sports.How is the talent identification process conducted under NSTS?The talent identification process typically involves scouting at the school and district levels, where young athletes are assessed based on their skills, physical fitness, and potential for excellence in sports.What kind of support is provided to identified talents under the National Sports Talent Search?Identified talents may receive various forms of support, including training programs, coaching, sports equipment, and opportunities to compete at higher levels to enhance their skills.How to apply for a scheme?
