Dependents of untimely dead due to famine, floods, excessive rains, fires and accidental accidents help. For treatment information - Assistance is accepted in recognized private hospitals from the major Government/ Chief Minister s Relief Fund of the state. 40% maximum amount of rs1.50 lakh in government hospitals and 30% maximum amount of Rs 90,000 in recognized private hospitals, whichever is less, is accepted and sent to the concerned hospital. BenefitsDemand draft or Direct Bank Transfer to the beneficiary.Cochlear Implant Surgery of Deaf and Dumb Children - In the state of Rajasthan, deaf and dumb children up to the age of 6 years, whose family income is up to Rs. 2.00 lakh annually,Assistance for Thalassemia Major Disease Treatment - 2 percent assistance is given from the Chief Minister's Assistance Fund up to Rs. 50.7 lakhFinancial assistance to a person suffering from serious diseases (cancer, kidney and urine, heart disease) do it. EligibilityThe applicant should be Non BPLThe Annal Income of the Family should be greater than 2 LPANot be listed in ABMGRSBY (Ayushman Bharat Mahatma Gandhi Rajasthan Swasthya Bima Yojana) Application ProcessOnlineOfflineVisit the official website of the Chief Minister Relief Fund Rajasthan at https://cmrf.rajasthan.gov.in/. Click on the "Apply for Medical Help" option on the homepage.You will be directed to a new page where you need to select the "Apply Online" option.Fill in the required details such as your name, address, contact details, medical condition, and required treatment.Upload the necessary documents such as medical certificates, doctor's reports, and bills.Once you have filled in all the details, click on the "Submit" button.After submitting the application, you will receive an acknowledgment number that you can use to track the status of your application. Individual from Non BPL whose family income should be atleast 2lakhs per annumWhat is the objective of this scheme?Rajasthan Chief Minister s Relief Fund medical help scheme launched on April 1999 under Chief Minister s Relief Fund in Rajasthan for the Dependents of untimely dead due to famine, floods, excessive rains, fires and accidental accidents help. How it will help for treatment at hospital?Assistance is accepted in recognized private hospitals from the major Government/ Chief Minister s Relief Fund of the state. 40% maximum amount of rs1.50 lakh in government hospitals and 30% maximum amount of Rs 90,000 in recognized private hospitals, whichever is less, is accepted and sent to the concerned hospital.How this scheme help to get medical help?1. One time full payment by Demand draft or Direct bank transfer 2. Cochlear Implant Surgery of Deaf and Dumb Children - In the state of Rajasthan, deaf and dumb children up to the age of 6 years, whose family income is up to Rs. 2.00 lakh annually, 3. Assistance for Thalassemia Major Disease Treatment - 2 percent assistance is given from the Chief Minister's Assistance Fund up to Rs. 50.7 lakh 4. Financial assistance to a person suffering from serious diseases (cancer, kidney and urine, heart disease) do it. Who is eligible for this scheme?1. The applicant should be Non BPL 2. The Annal Income of the Family should be greater than 2 LPA 3. Not be listed in ABMGRSBY Which documents are required to apply for this scheme?1. Income Certificate Copy* 2. Address Proof Copy* 3. Rashan Card 4. Treatment Estimate by Doctor 5. Medical Certificate How to apply for this scheme1. Visit the official website of the Chief Minister Relief Fund Rajasthan at https://cmrf.rajasthan.gov.in/. 2. Click on the "Apply for Medical Help" option on the homepage. 3. You will be directed to a new page where you need to select the "Apply Online" option. 4. Fill in the required details such as your name, address, contact details, medical condition, and required treatment. 5. Upload the necessary documents such as medical certificates, doctor's reports, and bills. 6. Once you have filled in all the details, click on the "Submit" button. 7. After submitting the application, you will receive an acknowledgment number that you can use to track the status of your application. Alternatively, you can also apply offline by downloading the application form from the website and submitting it along with the necessary documents to the Chief Minister Relief Fund office.
