Where to applyChief Executive Offcer, Janpad Panchayat and Commissioner/Chief Municipal Officer, Urban Local Body Documents RequiredApplication form in the prescribed format along with a copy of the registration card, Certificate given by the District Superintendent of Police and District Sports Officer through the sports organization.Construction workers and their family members holding valid
