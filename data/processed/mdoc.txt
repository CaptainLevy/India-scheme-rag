The scheme has the objective of providing coaching to the un-successful Madhyamik ST students who could not succeed due to various reasons of inconveniences. Special coaching is being provided to the Madhyamik failed ST students for a period of 7 (seven) months through NGO run Coaching centres.. Stipend @ Rs. 65/- per student per day is provided to the hostellers.BenefitsSpecial coaching is being provided to the Madhyamik failed ST students for a period of 7 (seven) months through NGO run Coaching centres..
