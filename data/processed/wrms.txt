The primary objective of this scheme is to help in the rehabilitation of widows after remarriage. Widow Re-Marriage Scheme launched by the Ministry of Women and Child Development, Government of Himachal Pradesh. The main purpose of the scheme is to help in the rehabilitation of widows, by encouraging men to enter into wedlock with widows. This scheme provides financial assistance to such widows. Under this scheme financial assistance of Rs. 25, 000 is provided out of which cash grant of Rs. 10,000 will be given to widow at the time of marriage and Rs. 15,000 will be kept in the form of Fixed Deposit jointly at least for five years. The initiative has helped many destitute widows of the state. All widow permanent residents of Himachal can apply to the scheme. More details and application form can be obtained at nearest District welfare office, the Government of Himachal Pradesh.
