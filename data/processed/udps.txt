The dwarf person has to face difficulty in living his life in a social environment. Sometimes, it s not easy for getting employment for Dwarf people. The main purpose of the scheme is to provide pensions and connect the dwarf people of the Uttarakhand state to the mainstream of society. The main aim of this scheme is to provide social security to the Dwarf people of the state who are unable to sustain themselves from their own resources. So, this scheme will provide a better livelihood and make their life easy. In this scheme, dwarf below 4 feet can eligible for getting benefits of the pension. The Uttarakhand government took a very good initiative for the Dwarf people.BenefitsBenefitsThe government provides financial assistance of Rs. 1200/- per month. EligibilityEligibility Applicant height should be below 4 feet for getting benefits of the Dwarf Pension Scheme.The age should be 21 years or more, and the benefit of any other scheme is not being provided under the social security program.Applicant should be a resident of Uttarakhand state.There is no provision for a monthly income limit for eligibility under this scheme.Application ProcessOfflineApplication ProcessVisit the official website.Download the application form the official website. Fill out the application form with the required details.Attach the necessary documents.Applicants should visit Social Welfare Department in the concerned District/Taluka office to submit the application form.
