The objective of the scheme is to generate interest among the children of the fishermen community cash award incentive to the pass outs of HSC examination will be provided for higher education after admission into the courses. The scheme provides scholarship of upto 7500/- based on the Percentage of marks secured in HSC examination.BenefitsAmount of the Scholarship1. Percentage of Marks Secured in HSC Examination: 70% and above; Cash Award (in ): 7,500/-2. Percentage of Marks Secured in HSC Examination: 60% and above up to 69%; Cash Award (in ): 5,000/-3. Percentage of Marks Secured in HSC Examination: 50% and Above up to 59%; Cash Award (in ): 3,000/- Number of SlotsThe Budget provision under the scheme is to provide scholarship to 5510 numbers of meritorious children of fisher community. Mode of PaymentAfter admission in to higher course, the award will be awarded once to a child via DBT. This assistance will be extended over and above the prevailing scope by the School & Mass Education Department.EligibilityThe applicant must be a bonafide permanent resident of Odisha.The father of the applicant must be a Licensed Fisherman in the state of Odisha.The applicant must have passed the Higher Secondary School Examination.The applicant must have secured at least 50% marks.The school in which the applicant is admitted into must be recognized by the Govt. of Odisha.The applicant must not be in receipt of any scholar or stipend except merit scholarship, from any sources.Application ProcessOfflineStep 1: The child of the fisherman shall submit application in plain paper with copy of HSC Pass certificate and mark sheet and College Admission receipt through Asst. Fisheries Officers / Block Development Officer of the Block conceded to the District Fisheries Officers.Step 2: The verification will be entrusted to the respective AFO or field functionaries who will verify the genuineness of the certificate ascertaining from the respective Headmaster of the School.Step 3: The Headmaster of the High School will submit the list of eligible students to Asst. Fisheries Officers to facilitate selection.Documents Required1. HSC Passing Certificate and Marksheet 2. Caste Certificate3. Occupation Certificate (Affidavit) with Income Proof4. College Admission Receipt5. Bank Passbook Xerox6. Aadhar Card Xerox7.
