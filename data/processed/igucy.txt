Through this scheme, a scholarship of 18,000/- per annum will be awarded to 10 meritorious students each from Class 12 (Arts, Science, and Commerce) and from the merit list of B.A., B.Sc. and B.Com. degree. The scholarship will be renewed yearly until they complete the degree/course.BenefitsA scholarship of of 18,000/- per annum.Eligibility1. The applicant should be a resident of Himachal Pradesh.2. The applicant should have passed Class 12 in Science/Arts/Commerce.3. The applicant should have secured 60% or more marks in class 12th.4. The applicant should be a merit list holder from the Himachal Pradesh Board of School Education, Dharamshala.5. The applicant should have been admitted in an academic/professional course. Application ProcessOnlineStep 1: Go to the Official Website of the National Scholarship Portal. and click New Registration . Guidelines for Registration will appear. Scroll to the bottom. Read carefully the undertaking. Accept the Terms. Click Continue . Step 2: A Registration Form will appear. The fields marked as * are mandatory. Fill in the details and click Register . Your Application ID and password will be displayed. The same will also be sent as an SMS to your registered mobile number. Step 3: Go to https://scholarships.gov.in/fresh/newstdRegfrmInstruction Click on Login to Apply . Enter your Application ID and password. Type the Captcha and click Login . On the next screen, provide the OTP received on your registered mobile number. You will be directed to the Password Reset screen. Create a new password and confirm. Click Submit . You will be directed to the Applicant s Dashboard . Step 4: On the left pane, click Application Form . The fields marked as * are compulsory. Fill in the details and upload the documents. You can either click on Save as Draft to complete the application later. Else, click Final Submit to submit the application. NOTE: All the applicants are advised to fill the form correctly, no changes can be made once submitted. The applicants are also advised to fill in only one application as multiple applications will cancel the form.Documents Required1. Passport Size Photo.2. Aadhaar Card (Unique Identification Number UID/ Enrollment Identification Number EID)3. Himachal Bonafide Certificate.4. Previous Year(s) Result Card(s) from Matric onwards.5. Latest Bank Statement of the Student's Bank Account.Frequently Asked QuestionsCan you provide the link for scheme-specific documents required?Scheme-specific documents are detailed at https://hpepass.cgg.gov.in/NewHomePage.do? actionParameter=stateSchemes. What happens if a student fails to achieve the required marks for scholarship renewal?Scholarship renewal is contingent on maintaining at least 55% marks in subsequent classes; failure to do so may result in non-renewal. What is the rate of scholarship under this scheme?The scholarship rate is 10,000 per annum per student. Where can one find the detailed guidelines for the "Indira Gandhi Utkrisht Chattervriti Yojana"?The comprehensive guidelines are available at https://scholarships.gov.in/public/schemeGuidelines/HP_state_scheme/Indira_Gandhi_Utkrisht_Chhatravriti_Yojna-merged-202324.pdf. How is the scholarship amount distributed among the selected students?The scholarship amount is 18,000 per annum, awarded to the top 10 students each from Class 12 and the merit list of B.A., B.Sc., and B.Com. Are multiple applications allowed for this scholarship? No, applicants are advised to submit only one application as multiple submissions will lead to cancellation. What documents are needed for the application process?Required documents include Passport Size Photo, Aadhaar Card, Himachal Bonafide Certificate, Previous Result Cards, and the Student's Bank Account Statement. What are the eligibility criteria for applicants under this scheme?Eligibility requires residency in Himachal Pradesh, a minimum 60% marks in Class 12, and admission to an academic or professional course. What is the objective of the "Indira Gandhi Utkrisht Chattervriti Yojana for Post Plus Two Students" scholarship scheme?The scheme aims to provide a scholarship of 18,000 per annum to meritorious students pursuing academic or professional courses in Himachal Pradesh. Is there any application fee or cost associated with applying for this scheme?The entire application process is entirely free of cost. There are no application fees.Is It Mandatory to Have a Bank Account for This Scheme?Yes, possession of a valid bank account is a mandatory requirement for participation in this scheme. All scheme benefits are disbursed directly to the beneficiary's registered bank account.Can Applicants From The Neighboring States Also Apply To This Scheme?This scheme is exclusively available to permanent residents of the state.
