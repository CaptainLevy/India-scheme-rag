Mukhyamantri Bayan Jyoti Yojana OkYou need to before applying for schemesCancelSign In OdishaMukhyamantri Bayan Jyoti YojanaBayan JyotiMukhyamantriDetailsObjective :To provide required illumination for weaving on the loom/for illumination of the preparatory workplaces for bobbin winding, tie-dye making etc, the shed and one fan for smooth working. To facilitate carrying his weaving activity during late evening hours/failure of electricity/low voltage. Improve the health of the weaver by reducing physical strain on the eye and also fatigue.Allow the weaver to work for more time for higher production resulting in better earnings. Funding Pattern The unit will cost around Rs 17,500/- only.This unit cost may change periodically and is subject to review by Government.100% of the cost of the unit will be borne by the State GovernmentBenefitsBenefitsThe government is going to distribute 20 watts of tube lights free of cost, for this you will not have to pay any money.The second advantage of this Bayana Jyoti Yojana is that the workers will also be given 17500 free of cost to buy inverters under this scheme, which the Odisha government will give money for.The third advantage of this Bayana Jyoti Yojana is that a fan of 35 watts will be given to the beneficiary officers, whose money will be given by the Orissa government itself and all the beneficiaries will get a fan of 35 watts.4th Benefit: Its last and most important benefit is that under the Ballia scheme, they will be given a loan of up to 5000, in which no interest rate will be charged to them, that is, there will be no improvement in it. EligibilityEligibility He/She must be a bonafide weaver / full-time ancillary worker. if. He/She is a permanent resident/ domicile of Odisha.The family income from all sources does not exceed Rs.3, 00,000k per annum.At least 50% income of the family should be from weaving activities.He/She must not have availed such type of benefits earlier from the State Government / Central Government / any Government Agency.He or she should purchase the lighting unit from a GST-registered dealer with valid TIN.Where more than one member of a family is found eligible, only one of them will be entitled to benefit under this scheme Application ProcessOnlineApplication in the prescribed format shall be collected from the eligible weaver-household (Head of the Family) in online mode through the website of the Directorate. First Applicant needs to create an account and credentials. Now login with earlier details.Select the schemes under the scheme section and provide the details (Personal Details, Address Details, Income/Bank/Electricity Details, Document upload).SubmitIn case of non-availability of online facilities in remote areas, the concerned Zonal Officers will collect the applications from the beneficiaries and upload the same in the Online Portal of the Directorate.Documents RequiredList of documents to be submitted along with the application form.Copy of recent passport-size color photograph.Self-attested copy of the Weavers Identity Card or certificate that he is a bonafide weaver or ancillary worker.Self-attested copy of AADHAR card.Self-attested Xerox copy of the 1st page of the individual Savings Bank Pass Bock showing photograph, full name, name of father/spouse, address, bank, branch, account number, IFSC and MICR code details into which the assistance will be deposited. Account must be in the name of the applicant as submitted in the application form. Account must be opened in a Bank with a core banking facility to enable the digital transfer of assistance.Certificate regarding total annual family income from the concerned Tahasildar.An affidavit that he/she is not in receipt of similar assistance from either the State or Central Government.An undertaking to the effect that the beneficiary will not sell off the lighting unit within three years from the date of purchase.Frequently Asked QuestionsWho are eligible for MBJY ?1.
