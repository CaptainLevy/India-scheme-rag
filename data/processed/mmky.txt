Under this scheme, daughters of families below the poverty line, widows, destitute women, and families with an annual income of less than Rs. 35,000 are eligible for financial assistance from the government for their marriage. The scheme aims to reduce the financial burden on families during the marriage of their daughters and promote gender equality. The scheme has been successful in providing financial support to many families, particularly those from underprivileged backgrounds, and helping them to celebrate the marriage of their daughters with dignity and joy.Benefits1. Financial assistance for the marriage of daughters.2. Reducing the financial burden on families during marriage.3. Promoting gender equality.4.
