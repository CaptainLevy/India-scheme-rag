Under the scheme, financial assistance of 2,000/- will be provided to differently-abled mothers coming under the purview of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities (RPwD) Act 2016. As per the scheme, financial assistance will be provided to differently-abled mothers until the child turns two years for the nurturing of the baby after delivery and for the preparation of basic amenities. The scheme is implemented as per G.O.(Rt)No.268-2020-SJD dated 23-06- 2020.BenefitsUnder the scheme, financial assistance of 2,000/- will be provided to differently-abled mothers until the child turns two years.EligibilityThe applicant with the following type of disability will be considered under the scheme:Sl.
