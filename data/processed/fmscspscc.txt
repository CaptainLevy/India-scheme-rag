Fodder Minikits under Schedule Castes Sub Plan (SC Category) OkYou need to before applying for schemesCancelSign In GujaratFodder Minikits under Schedule Castes Sub Plan (SC Category)FarmerFodder MinikitsScheduled CasteSeedDetailsThe scheme Fodder Minikits under Schedule Castes Sub Plan is implemented by the Directorate of Animal Husbandry, Agriculture, Farmers Welfare and Co-operation Department, Government of Gujarat. Under this scheme, improved varieties of fodder seeds are provided to beneficiaries for widespread publicity, demonstration, and adaptation by farmers.BenefitsUnder this scheme, the fodder seed minikit of 3 Kg, 7 Kg, or 10 Kg is given to the eligible beneficiary.EligibilityThe beneficiary should be a permanent resident of Gujarat.The beneficiary of the Scheduled Caste category can avail the benefit under this scheme.The beneficiary should possess 10 Guntha of land to his or her name. Application ProcessOfflineTo avail the benefits of the scheme, the scheduled caste beneficiary may contact a nearby Intensive Cattle Development Project Sub Centre.Documents Required1. Identity proof2. Proof of Residence3. Proof of possessing10 Guntha of land4.
