Girls of all classes can take advantage of the scheme. The amount of this scheme will be given every year on Basant Panchami. The girl students who will get the benefit of the scheme in class 10th will be required to enrol in the school in classes 11th and 12th. And the government will give the amount to the beneficiary girl child through a check, so it is mandatory for the girl child to have a bank account. You can apply for the scheme online.BenefitsPayable for 11 & 12 class girl students: Rs. 3,000/- each year on the day of Basant Panchami. EligibilityThe student must have secured minimum 75% marks in class 10th.The girl student should be studying regularly in class 11 or 12.There is no restriction on income, occupation and caste etc. of the parents of the student.Application ProcessOnlineScholarship Application online through School Principal on Shaladarpan Portal.Click on "Balika Shiksha Protsahan" or click here. Select the scheme and click on apply button. Now select the scheme name. Fill out the required details and process further. Documents RequiredEducation Qualification Marksheet / Certificate Copy.Aadhar Card Copy.Bank Passbook Copy.Jan Aadhar/Bhamashah Card Copy.Frequently Asked QuestionsWhat is the scope of this scheme?The benefit of Gargi Puraskar Yojana is given every year to the beneficiary girl child of Rajasthan on the day of Basant Panchami. This time also the beneficiary girl students were taken on a tour of the girl students on the occasion of Basant Panchami on January 29, 2020.What are the benefits under the scheme ?Payable for 11 & 12 class girl students: Rs. 3,000/- each year on Basant Panchami. Who can get the benefits of the scheme ?All Rajasthan State, students who full fill the eligibility criteria. Is there caste restriction for students ?No, there is no such restriction. What Is The Education Qualification For The Scheme ? The Girl student must have secured a minimum of 75% marks in class 10th and studying regularly in class 11 or 12.Is only gril student get the scheme benefits ?Yes, Girl students will be eligible. What is the financial eligibility for applicant ?There is no restriction on income.How To Apply For The Scheme ?1. Scholarship Application online through School Principal on Shaladarpan Portal. 2. Click on "Balika Shiksha Protsahan" or click here. 3. Select the scheme and click on apply button. 4. Now select the scheme name. 5. Fill out the required details and process further. What Documents Are Needed ?1. Education Qualification Marksheet / Certificate Copy. 2. Aadhar Card Copy. 3. Bank Passbook Copy. 4. Jan Aadhar/Bhamashah Card Copy.What is the official web portal to apply ?Use the following URL.
