Under the scheme, the registered construction worker is providing nutritious meals at concessional rates at the workplace or on Kadianaka. Registered construction workers and their family members can get the benefit.BenefitsUnder this scheme, healthy and nutritious meals will be provided to the construction workers at a concessional rate of 5/- per meal.EligibilityThe benefits of this scheme can be availed by construction workers registered with Gujarat Building and Other Construction Worker s Welfare Board and their family members.Application ProcessOffline The registered construction worker is about providing nutritious meals at the workplace or on Kadianaka.
