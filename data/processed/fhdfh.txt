Financial assistance is provided so that the family does not face financial burden immediately after the death of the head of the family and his family can be helped. Scheme Delivery Time :180 DaysDesignated Officer of Scheme :ADDITIONAL DISTRICT MAGISTRATEFINANCIAL HELP ON DEATH OF FAMILY HEAD is implemented in the following District :Baran BenefitsFinancial Assistance of 10,000 to the wife of the deceased head of the family is provided.Eligibility1. The person must be from Sahariya Tribe (ST)Application ProcessOfflineThe application form of the scheme can be obtained from and submitted to the Additional Collector and Project Officer, Shahbad. Documents RequiredDeath CertificateIdentity Proof : AadhaarBank Passbook : Bank Passbook PhotocopyAddress Proof : Ration cardCaste Certificate Frequently Asked QuestionsWho is this scheme for?This scheme is for the Sahariya Tribe people of Rajasthan.Is this an individual or a family scheme?This is a family scheme where the wife of the deceased head of the family gets benefit for taking care of the immediate needs of the family.How can a wife apply for this scheme? The application form of the scheme can be obtained from and submitted to the Additional Collector and Project Officer, Shahbad.Is this scheme available only for the residents of Shahbad?Not just Shahbad, this scheme is available for the residents of whole Baran District.Which documents are required to get the benefits under this scheme?The following documents are required for availing the Benefits under this scheme Death Certificate Aadhaar Bank Passbook Copy Ration Card Caste CertificateWhat is the amount of money that the wife of deceased head of the family receives under this scheme?Under this scheme, the wife of the deceased receives a sum of 10,000.How does the beneficiary receive the money under this scheme?Since it is a direct benefit transfer scheme, the beneficiary receives the amount in their bank account.Sources And ReferencesScheme DetailsOkWas this helpful?News and UpdatesNo new news and updates availableShare OkYou need to before applying for schemesCancelSign In RajasthanFinancial Help On Death Of Family HeadDBTDeath Of Family HeadFinancial AssistanceRajasthanSahariya TribeDetailsBenefitsEligibilityApplication ProcessDocuments RequiredFrequently Asked QuestionsFinancial help on death of family head scheme was launched by Rajasthan state government under tribal area development on 20/08/2008. 10,000/- to the wife of the deceased on the premature death of the head of the family of Saharia primitive tribe.
