Schedule Tribe Pre-Metric Scholarship Class 06 To 08 OkYou need to before applying for schemesCancelSign In RajasthanSchedule Tribe Pre-Metric Scholarship Class 06 To 08EducationFinancial AssistanceLearningStudent FinanceDetailsThe Rajasthan government aims to provide educational opportunities to students belonging to Schedule Tribe student, specifically those studying in grades 6 to 8. This program targets students from Schedule Tribe whose parents/guardians are not income taxpayers and not getting any other type of scholarship or allowance for study from central, state / public sources.BenefitsFor Schedule Caste Boy Students Rs. 75/- per month.For Schedule Caste Girl Students Rs. 125/- per month.EligibilityThe student should belong to the Scheduled Tribe category.The student who is studying as a regular student in classes 6 to 8 in schools run by the Central Government, State Government, Municipalities and schools recognized by the Department of Education.Students whose parents/guardians are not income taxpayers when they are not alive.The student who is not getting any other type of scholarship or allowance for study from central, state / public sources.The student who has not failed in the lower class in the last session. If he fails in any year's examination, his scholarship will be stopped, but if he fails in the same class in the nextIf he passes the examination, he will be eligible for the scholarship in the new class from the next session. For this, the student will have to apply for a new scholarship again.Students who are not living in hostels are run or aided by the Department of Social Justice and Empowerment.Application ProcessOnlineApplicant to log in to RajSSO Portal using his/her SSOID.If SSOID is not available then the applicant to first register on RajSSO Portal to create SSOID and update SSO Profile at first login.After successful authentication/login and updation of the SSOID Profile and select the scheme name. Select his/her name, the bank account and fill in personal details.Upload the required document.Clicking the SUBMIT button. Documents RequiredAadhaar Card.Annual Income Of The Parent / Guardian - Non-Tax Payee.Janaadhaar Card Details Of Family And Student.Bank Details Linked With Janaadhaar Card Of Either The Family Head Or Student.Caste Certificate.Birth Certificate.Frequently Asked QuestionsWhat is this scheme?In the year 1984, Rajasthan Government started a scholarship to encourage the education of the Schedule Tribe.What are the benefits under the scheme ?1. Rs. 75/- per month to Male Scheduled Tribe students studying in classes 6 to 8. 2. Rs. 125/- per month to Female Scheduled Tribe students studying in classes 6 to 8 Who can get the benefits of the scheme ?The student should belong to the Scheduled Tribe category and full fill the eligibility criteria. Is other than Scheduled Tribe (ST) student get the benefits of the scheme ?No, The student should belong to the Scheduled Tribe (ST) category only.What Is The Education Qualification For The Scheme ?The student who is studying as a regular student in classes 6 to 8 in schools run by the Central Government, State Government, Municipalities and schools recognized by the Department of Education.Can other education scheme beneficiaries also get benefits from this scheme?No, Other Education Scheme Beneficiaries will not be considered. What is the financial eligibility for applicant ?Student guardians should not be income taxpayers.How To Apply For The Scheme ?1. Applicant to log in to RajSSO Portal using his/her SSOID. 2. After successful authentication/login and updation of the SSOID Profile and select the scheme name. 3. Select his/her name, the bank account and fill in personal details. 4. Upload the required documents. 5. Click on the SUBMIT button.What Documents Are Needed ?1. Aadhaar Card. 2. Annual Income Of The Parent / Guardian - Non-Tax Payee. 3. Janaadhaar Card Details Of Family And Student. 4. Bank Details Linked With the Janaadhaar Card Of Either The Family Head Or Student. 5. Caste Certificate. 6. Birth Certificate.What is the official web portal to apply ?Use the following link.
