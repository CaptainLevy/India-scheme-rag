Under this scheme, pregnant women will get the financial assistance that will help her during the pregnancy expenses . BenefitsFinancial Assistance : 10,000/-EligibilityApplicant should be a permanent resident of Chhattisgarh.Benefit will only be provided for only first two children.Benefit will only be provided to registered women.Registration as a construction worker is required one year in advance.It is mandatory to have authorized verification of pregnancy of the female worker by a doctor, ANM or Mitanin.Maternity assistance benefits will not be provided to the spouse of a construction worker employed in public and government institutions.Construction worker beneficiaries without valid Board membership will not qualify for the Maternity Assistance Scheme benefits.Application ProcessOfflineFirstly, Applicant have to register at labor office of the area.Get the application form and full fill the form with required detailsAttach the required documents. The application will have to be submitted within the time limit to same office.Documents RequiredBirth certificate of the child.A scanned copy of the registration certificate.Mobile Number. Aadhaar Card.Self-declarationBank passbook photo copy.Frequently Asked QuestionsWhat is this scheme?Chhattisgarh State Government started a scheme "Bhagini Prasuti Sahayata Yojana" for pregnant women. What is the benefits under this scheme ?Financial benefits are provided. What is the monetary sum offered or provided?Amount : 10,000/-Who will get the scheme benefits?1. Registration of the beneficiary's husband or wife is necessary. 2. It is mandatory to have authorized verification of pregnancy of the female worker by a doctor, ANM or Mitanin. 3. Wives of construction workers working in public and government institutions will not benefit from the scheme. 4. The benefit of maternity scheme will be payable for maximum two deliveries only. May other state women get the benefits?No, this is only for Chhattisgarh State. How late can a applicant apply for the scheme?Applicant can apply up to 90 days after the birth of the childFor how many children can the applicant avail the benefit of this scheme?Only upto 2 children. How will the beneficiary get the benefit?Beneficiary will get the benefit through the RTGS/NEFT in her bank account.Who will get the benefit if a woman dies during childbirth?Benefits will be provided to her husband. What documents are required for registration?Birth certificate of the child. A scanned copy of the registration certificate. Mobile Number. Aadhaar Card. Self-declaration.
