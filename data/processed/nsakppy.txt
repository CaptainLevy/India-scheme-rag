This scheme will be effective on those employed in the Labour Department building and other construction workers who are registered as beneficiaries in the board under Section-12 of the The Building and Other Construction Workers (Regulation of Employment and Conditions of Service) Act, 1996, and are depositing their contributions regularly. This scheme will be effective in the entire state of Rajasthan. International level sports competition to the eligible beneficiary or his children under this scheme Incentive amount will be given as follows: -On participating in the competition- 2,00,000/- RupeesOn receiving the Bronze medal- 5,00,000/- RupeesOn receiving the Silver medal- 8,00,000/- RupeesOn receiving the Gold medal- 11,00,000/- Rupees Conditions for eligibility to apply under this scheme: Applicant should be a resident of Rajasthan State.For this scheme, those construction workers will be eligible, who are registered as labour under labour law. In addition to the above, unmarried sons and daughters of construction workers will also be eligible for assistance under the scheme.The provisions of the scheme will not be applicable for any online gaming / betting-related competition.Declaration of invalidity of Sportsperson or withdrawal of medals won by the participant due to doping or any valid reason stipulated by organising committee no incentives will be payable under the scheme.During scrutiny of applications and documents, if any facts are found to be false resulting in the cancellation of eligibility, it will be the responsibility of the concerned beneficiary to deposit all the assistance amount sanctioned under the scheme along with interest. Application:The application has to be submitted by the beneficiary on Board's online portal www.ldms.rajasthan.gov.in in the form prescribed. Approval and selection of the candidate will be done by the senior-most officer of the labour office or the competent authority appointed by the secretary after the application has been examined.Time period for submission of application - Application can be submitted within a period of 6 months from the date of completion of International Sports Competition/Tournament.The incentive amount will be transferred through electronic means (RTGS / NEFT) to the candidate's bank account under the central banking system BenefitsThe incentive amount under this scheme for International level sports competition to the eligible beneficiary or his children will be given as follows: -Participating in the competition- 2,00,000/- RupeesOn receiving the Bronze medal- 5,00,000/- RupeesOn receiving the Silver medal- 8,00,000/- RupeesOn receiving the Gold medal- 11,00,000/- Rupees EligibilityApplicant should be a resident of Rajasthan State.For this scheme, those construction workers will be eligible, who are registered as labour under labour law. Those building and other construction workers will be eligible who are registered as beneficiaries in the board under Section-12 of The Building and Other Construction Workers (Regulation of Employment and Conditions of Service) Act, 1996 and are depositing their contribution regularly.In addition to the above, unmarried sons and daughters of construction workers will also be eligible for assistance under the scheme. Application ProcessOnlineStep 01: The beneficiary will have to fill out the online form in the prescribed formatStep 02: Approval and selection of the candidate will be done by the senior-most officer of the labour office or the competent authority appointed by the secretary after the application has been examined. Note: The Application can be submitted within a period of 6 months from the date of completion of the International Sports Competition/Tournament. Documents Required1. Copy of the beneficiary registration identity card.2. Copy of the beneficiary's Aadhaar and Jan Aadhaar card.3. First page of the beneficiary savings bank account passbook (in which the beneficiary name, bank account number and IFS code are mentioned).4. Copy of the certificate issued by the International Sports Organizing Committee / Federation / Association / Council. The beneficiary needs to fill the online form in the prescribed format on www.ldms.rajasthan.gov.in along with the copies of the documents. The selection committee will examine the application and the amount under this scheme will be disbursed directly in the bank account of the beneficiary.Can a person apply for this scheme offline?No, only the online applications filled on Www.Ldms.Rajasthan.Gov.In are entertained.Who decides and approves the selection of the beneficiary under this scheme?Approval and selection of the candidate is done by the senior-most officer of the labour office or the competent authority appointed by the secretary after the application has been examined.
