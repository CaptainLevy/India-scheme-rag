Under this scheme, the government provides financial assistance of Rs. 1200 per month for disabled women /men who are engaged in agriculture work or agriculture-related business in the state. This scheme is effective from the 1st April 2014 and implemented by the social welfare department in Uttarakhand. The name of the scheme Teelu Rauteli was a female warrior and folk hero who lived in Gurad Village (Pargana Chaundkot) Garhwal, Uttrakhand. During the 17th century, she fought in seven wars between the ages of fifteen and twenty.BenefitsBenefitsThe financial assistance of Rs. 1200 per month is for disabled women /men who are engaged in agriculture work or agriculture-related business in the state. EligibilityEligibility Description Applicant should be from an agricultural business in rural area.The applicant's age should be between 18 to 60.Applicant should be a permanent resident of Uttarakhand Applicant should have disability between 20 to 40%. Application ProcessOnlineApplication ProcessApplicant should visit the official site. Select "Teelu Rauteli Special Pension" Scheme and thenClick on the option of the application form and download this.Now fill out the form and enclose all the required documentsAfter checking all the information and documents in the application form, submit it to the concerned department.Applicant should visit Social Welfare Department in the concerned District/Taluka or Agriculture Officer or Gram Panchayat. Who will get the benefit of Teelu Rauteli Special Pension Scheme?The benefit of the scheme will be admissible to such rural farmer women and men of uttarakhand, whose percentage of disability is between 20 to 40.What are the benefits of the Teelu Rauteli Special Pension Scheme ?Under Teelu Rauteli Special Pension Scheme, eligible people get an incentive of Rs.1200 every month Who can apply for the Teelu Rauteli Special Pension Scheme ?1. This assistance is being provided in the special situation of women involved in the agriculture business in rural areas and men in agriculture. Therefore, due to the already implemented scheme, the name of this scheme has been given as "Tilu Shastanteli Special Pension Scheme". 2. There is no provision for an income limit in this monthly scheme and the scheme is applicable from April 01, 2014. 3. The benefit of the scheme will be admissible to such rural women and men, who are between 20 to 40 years of a present disability. 4. The age of the person/applicant should be more than 18 years and up to 60 years and the benefit of any other scheme is not being provided for social security programs. 5. If such persons attain the age of 60 years and at that time they come under old age pension eligibility, then they can be considered based on the idea of an old age pension account and this facility.How to apply for Teelu Rauteli Special Pension Scheme To apply inTeelu Rauteli Special Pension Scheme, you will have to download the application form of the scheme by visiting the official website. After filling it, you have to submit it to the concerned department along with all the necessary documents.
